New Myth Generator 2022-

So far it consists of 'Chapter 01' and 'Chapter 02'.

The video series ‘New myth generator’ establishes the hypothesis that ‘the nonlin- ear nature of myths allows modern people to endure in crisis and restore a dimen- sion of existence.’
The first step toward resisting the irreversible violence of history and solving the crisis we face is to recognize the current situation.
Perceiving the fantastic tyranny of the capital, based on the success stories dotted with free paradise and the proposition that history is irreversible, brings us a new beginning stage of ongoing resistance.
What does the present purgatory, that we cannot perceive, look like and what im- ages can we present? In the confusion we are experiencing with our bodies, how will the mythical characteristic work as a key? What can we learn from it?
Based on the analysis and research of similarities and differences in the way mythical non-historical and nonlinear characteristics worked on humans, and the way contemporary society exists and operates as a giant myth, this work raises questions about modern people’s historicism and seeks a way to dismantle colonial hegemony.
